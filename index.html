Move, Attack, Kill, Win

========================

Move : Left/Right click

Attack : Q W E R , Currently each general has only one ability Q, Except Yasuo_tt7 has full 4 abilitys

Zoom In/Out : Scroll mouse wheel

Pause/Play : ESC

========================

Hacker mode: P (on/off). When enabled, we will show the path of abilities and champions, make it easier for you to play

Mute all: O (on/off). When enabled, the AI champions will not attack you

Move Camera: SPACE (on/off). When enabled, the camera is free to move, you can move the cursor to the edges of the browser to move the view.
